# Harbor Portal - Overview Page Visualization Ideas

Status: Draft (for future discussion)

## 1. Transaction Volume Trends

- Line chart showing transfer count over time
- Granularity toggle: daily / weekly / monthly
- Filter by transfer type (on-ramp / off-ramp)
- Helps users identify business patterns and growth

## 2. Transfer Status Distribution

- Donut or horizontal bar chart
- Breakdown: completed, pending, failed, cancelled
- Quick visual health check of transfer pipeline

## 3. Commission Revenue

- Prominent number card showing period total
- Small trend line for historical comparison
- Period selector (this week / this month / custom range)

## 4. Currency Pair / Corridor Distribution

- Bar chart showing volume by currency pair (e.g., USD→USDT, COP→USDC)
- Identifies which corridors are most active
- Useful for business planning and fee optimization

## 5. Success Rate

- Single percentage metric with trend indicator
- Compare current period vs previous period
- Early warning signal for operational issues
